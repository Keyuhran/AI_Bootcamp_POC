Project Scope/Background

At Public Utilities Board (PUB), Singapore’s National Water Agency's Water Quality Department (WQD), we conduct water quality tests to ensure the safety of Singapore's water supply system. Besides testing works, we also take the lead to front internal and external water quality issues. We spend a considerable amount of time replying to queries involving water quality issues. Although we receive an average of 1-2 queries per day, we do not have a dedicated team to respond to these queries.

We conducted a review and determined that most of these queries fall under a few themes: (1) water quality parameters in the regulations and our water supply, (2) water quality testing, (3) potential collaborators, and (4) enquiry on the treatment technologies in PUB.

Objectives

We plan to implement a Large Language Model (LLM) to draft responses to water quality issues. The main idea is to reduce manhours and free up our staff to handle more complex/critical inquiries. A typical customer response takes around 4 manhours to draft and clear. With the Generative AI (GenAI) chatbot, turnaround time will be reduced to an average of 2 hours.

The data source includes: (1) water quality data from regulations and (2) SFA regulatory requirements, as well as an email archive. This chatbot aims to provide grammatically correct and contextually accurate draft replies for proofreading.

Data Source

  • Water quality data:
    • EPH (Water Suitable for Drinking) Regulations 2019
    • PUB Drinking Water Quality Report (July 2023 – June 2024)
    • SFA Code of Practice on Drinking Water Sampling and Safety Plans (2019)
    • WHO Guidelines for Drinking-water Quality (4th edition)
  • Email archives:
    • Enquiries on water testing, parameters, and regulations
    • Complaints or observations (e.g. discoloured water, scaling)
    • Queries on treatment methods: desalination, reservoirs, disinfection
    • False reports by third parties and outreach events
    • Innovation and collaboration proposals

This curated PoC database represents a wide range of common queries to showcase chatbot effectiveness.

Features

Security Features

Includes password protection, prompt injection mitigation, and secure code execution.

Contextual Search and Retrieval

Supports both direct text input and .msg file uploads. Uses NLP to understand user intent and retrieve relevant data. Maintains session state throughout interactions.

Response Generation

Uses a `full_workflow` pipeline to generate clear and contextual responses alongside original input for user review. Prioritizes professionalism and user experience.
